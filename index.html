My Background

My previous experience with develpoment is minimal. Going into this project, the only experience I had under my belt was gained last year, when I took the Programming class at Souhegan. This class covered an introduction to the programming language Python, known for being beginner-friendly. Being taught by John Ranta that year was a great experience and had me eager to learn more in the computer science field. It’s always intrigued me but I never ended up acting on that interest until my Junior year because I never quite knew where to start.


I chose pursue Advanced Programming the next year, and that class was way more individual and self-driven than I had anticipated. I was able to choose what programming language I wanted to learn and was in charge of how I learnt it. After going through a few ideas of what I wished to try and learn, I stumbled upon HTML. Trying to make my own website seemed like a neat way to expand my knowledge, so I took on the challenge. I didn’t realize it then, but it was going to be more tough and confusing than I thought. Whether this site shows absolutely everything I’ve learned in the most effective way or not, I’m still very pleased with my journey and what I’ve been able to learn.
